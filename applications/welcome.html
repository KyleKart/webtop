Welcome to Your Webtop!

Window Controls:

  • Left Side: A single-clickable ❌ icon is always present and closes the window.
  • Right Side: Contains three controls:
    • ➖ Minimize: Hides the window from view (minimizes it).
    • > Shade: Collapses the window to titlebar height. Will change to a down-pointing arrow ▼ when collapsed/shaded.
    • ➕ Maximize: Maximizes/restores the window to its full size.

Enjoy exploring your Webtop!
